Comparative vs. Superlative

1. Colosimo makes (good) tortilla of Madrid.

2. I am definitely (small) my sister.

3. The Ritz is (expensive) hotel in Paris.

4. Between those two shirts, the one on the left is (pretty).

5. Mrs. Gonzalez (good) teacher I've had in my life.

6. The cat is (big) my dog.

7. Travelling by train is (cheap) travelling by airplane.

8. Eddie Murphy is (funny) actor of all time.

9. Real Madrid didn't play (good) Manchester City in the Champions finals.

10. Italian people are so much (nice) French people.

11. The sellers in Decathlon are (polite) the ones in El Corte Inglés.

12. Portugal is the (close) country to Salamanca.

13. In the winter, Madrid is (cold) Barcelona.

14. The cotton shirts are (comfortable) the silk shirts.

15. The last book that Santiago Posteguillo wrote is (interesting) in his catalog.

16. To become a doctor, you need to be (intelligent) to become a nurse.

17. Paris is (beautiful) city of Europe.

18. It is (good) to stay at home if there is a storm.

19. Florentino Pérez is (powerful) business man in Madrid.

20. Medellín used to be (dangerous) city of the entire world in the 80's.

21. Lions are (intense) animals than tigers.

22. People from the Basque country are (sincere) individuals I have come across in Spain.

23. The beaches in Andalucia are (hot) the beaches in Valencia.

24. Elephants are (aggressive) alligators.

25. The nights in the North Pole are (dark) than in South America.
